Our Story
An Etobicoke Institution

Fresh-Up Cleaners has been a fixture on Bloor Street West in Etobicoke for over thirty years. The same husband-and-wife team has opened those doors every morning — not for the business of it, but because they genuinely love what they do.

That love shows up in the details: the way a collar comes back crisp, the care taken with a piece you've told them is irreplaceable, the honest conversation when something needs more time. They've built their reputation not on volume, but on the kind of trust that makes families come back for decades.

They aren't the biggest dry cleaner in the city. They don't need to be. They're yours.

— The Fresh-Up family, Bloor St W

Our Story

A Family Business Built on Trust and Care

For over six decades, Fresh-Up Cleaners has been a cornerstone of the West End Junction community. What began as a small, hardworking family operation has grown into one of Toronto's most beloved and trusted garment care establishments — not through advertising, but through word of mouth and results that speak for themselves.

The husband-and-wife team behind Fresh-Up brings the same dedication to every single garment, whether it's a workday shirt or a precious heirloom piece. Neighbours become regulars. Regulars become family. That's the Fresh-Up way.

When other cleaners say "can't be done," we say "bring it in." Our experience with delicate fabrics, stubborn stains, and vintage garments has earned us the loyalty of generations of Toronto families.
